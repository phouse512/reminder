WELCOME!

Social Reminders is a way to help keep your friends accountable without actually knowing what they need to get done!

1. Register for an account with a username and phone number!

2. Add items that you would like to be reminded of to your personal list

3. Have friends add the global phone number given so they can remind you of tasks!

4. Whenever one of your 'aliases' are texted to this number, you'll get reminded about one of the items you initially shared :D

5. Get your friend to sign up and return the favor :)

What are you waiting for?
